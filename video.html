CD Commercial

This commercial was one of the assigment I got from school, where I choose to make a cd commercial. I start with making a stop motion of the case opening and closing itself and make a zoom in to the cd.


Because the cd is a soundtrack to a game, I made the start as a game menu, where it choose a track to play. after you will see clips with a matching soundtrack to show what kinds of music there are and what mood they tell. At the end the case will close and the product picture will show at the end.
